Our Esteemed Instructors

Dr. Andjelita Zeng

Dr. Andjelita Zeng is a highly accomplished pianist and musician with a long list of credentials. Having attained her Doctorate from the Royal Conservatory of Brussels, she has taught in many institutes internationally including:

  1. 1. International Music School of Genval (Belgium)
  2. 2. Institute of Music Nusantara (Jakarta) (Principal)
  3. 3. Gloriamus Music School (Jakarta) (Principal)
  4. 4. Methodist School of Music (Singapore) (Head of Teaching)

Be it furthering their studies or to seek a deeper appreciation in music, Dr. Zeng’s deep-rooted passion drives her to help aspiring musicians achieve their goals. She finds satisfaction and shares the sense of achievement when her students’ hard work payoff be it attaining a distinction in the ABRSM exam, attaining their Fellowship Degree, or even performing in front of an audience for the very first time.


In her free time she maintains a healthy body and mind by hitting the gym, to declutter and to help her stay ready in leading Cephas.

Ms. Ethel Loh

Ms Ethel Loh has been teaching for the past 40 years. Possessing a degree in Early Childhood Education coupled with many years of experience, she is adept in engaging children! She enjoys blessing her little pianists with the right skills and mindset to overcome their obstacles as she showers them with love. She strives to help children enjoy and appreciate music through moving, listening, singing, playing and also creating (their own music)! Having fun while learning is important; for that, she believes would go a long way in developing a love for music, especially for the very young!


When not teaching, she devotes her time to the Lord in prayer and worship meetings.

Mrs. Ng Choy See

With more than 20 years of experience in teaching the piano, she has groomed many students into fine musicians. She passionately encourages her students to give their best and they leave her classroom happy and motivated to practice more!


Mrs. Ng had 12 years of experience in teaching piano and achieved Grade 8 in piano (practical and theory). She feels fulfilled when she teaches someone who is motivated and is committed to learn and put in hard work. She feels satisfied when she is able to impart knowledge or a skill to another person.

Mr. Roslan

With deep passion for the guitar, Mr Roslan always gives 101% when sharing his knowledge! With his warm personality, both adults and children are quick to open up to him. He loves it especially when his students are able to pluck and strum to their hearts’ content.


When not sharing his knowledge to his students, he shares his passion with his audience by playing in a live band on the weekends! He has also performed in formal functions for more than 10 years! Check him out here!
